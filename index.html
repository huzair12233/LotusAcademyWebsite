Our Salient features

  • Regular Lectures

    We provide daily lectures to help our students stay on track with their studies and reinforce their understanding of the course material. Our experienced teachers use modern teaching techniques to keep the students engaged and motivated

  • Regular Tests

    Several tests like Weekly Tests, Topic wise Tests and Full-Length Tests are conducted regularly during the course, to help students identify their weak areas and work on strengthening them.

  • Experienced & Qualified Staff

    We have a team of highly experienced professors who have developed a deep insight into the nature and trend of the board exams and engineering entrance exams. They are very friendly and approachable and make every effort to make the subject interesting for the students.

  • Personal Counselling Guidance

    We offer personalized counseling guidance to our students to help them with any academic or personal issues they may be facing. Our counselors are trained professionals who can help students overcome academic difficulties, improve their study habits, and manage stress and anxiety.

  • Affordable Fee Structures

    We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer affordable fee structures that are within the reach of all students

  • Limited Students Per Batch

    We limit the number of students per batch to ensure that each student gets personalized attention from our teachers. This allows us to tailor our teaching methods to the individual needs of each student and help them achieve their full potential.

  • A.C Classrooms

    Our classrooms are equipped with air conditioning to provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment for our students. We understand that students learn better when they are comfortable and relaxed.

  • CCTV Surveillance

    We have installed CCTV cameras in our classrooms to ensure the safety and security of our students. The cameras provide 24/7 surveillance and allow us to monitor the classrooms in real-time.

  • Biometric Attendance

    Online Biometric Attendance for all students. Permanent report card maintained for each student. Subject-wise attendance maintained. Absentees are notified on same day
